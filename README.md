# Abyssal_Frontier

You play as a prisoner, sent to dangerous depths to investigate a discovered craft in the deep ocean. You have limited oxygen and will only be taken back to the surface once you discover usefull information.

WASD: Walk
Tab: Invetory
E: Interact
Click in inventory: Inspect
X: Exit inspection

Your goal is to find 3 power cells and restart the ships power.

My advice in stage one was to make sure i could make an underwater scne look good before i commited to it, so my first focus in this project was to add water simulation above the player and develop a post porcess effect to make the screne look like it was being viewed through a diving helmet under water.
